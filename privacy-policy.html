This Privacy Policy explains how AnnAlchemy™ ("AnnAlchemy," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit annalchemy.org, take the Quick Skin Check, join a notify or email list, or purchase a guide. AnnAlchemy™ is a nurse-led, evidence-informed educational brand operated by Anna Cadano, RN, based in Taguig City, Philippines.

AnnAlchemy™ provides educational support only. It does not provide medical diagnosis, prescription, or treatment, and it does not replace care from a licensed dermatologist or healthcare provider.

Information we collect

We collect only what we need to provide our educational content and to communicate with you:

  • Contact information you provide directly, such as your name and email address when you join a list or purchase a guide.
  • Quick Skin Check responses, which may include general, non-clinical descriptions of your skin concerns. These are used to route you to the most relevant educational starting point. They are not a medical record and are not a diagnosis.
  • Purchase information processed through our third-party form and payment provider. We do not store your full payment card details on our own systems.
  • Basic technical information such as general usage data that helps the website function. Our site is hosted on GitHub Pages.

How we use your information

  • To deliver the guides, resources, or sessions you request or purchase.
  • To route you to the most relevant educational starting point based on your Quick Skin Check responses.
  • To send you the emails you have opted into, such as launch notifications and educational content.
  • To respond to your questions when you contact us.
  • To maintain the security and proper functioning of our website.

A note on sensitive information

Some information you choose to share, such as descriptions of recurring acne or skin concerns, may be considered sensitive. We treat this information with care. We ask that you do not send us detailed medical histories, diagnoses, photographs, or other clinical records through our forms or email, as these channels are not designed to store medical records. For anything requiring medical evaluation, please consult a licensed dermatologist or healthcare provider.

Third-party tools we rely on

We use trusted third-party services to operate AnnAlchemy™. These include our form and quiz provider (Tally), our website host (GitHub Pages), and our email and payment providers. Each of these has its own privacy practices, and we encourage you to review them. We share information with these providers only to the extent needed to deliver our services.

How we protect and retain your information

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you share with us, and we retain it only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy or as required by law. We keep your contact information for as long as you remain subscribed or until you ask us to remove it.
